About

Meet Doodle!

Doodle is here to represent the amount of time we spend procasinating through distractions like doodling and social media. With amazing lives to live, we don't want to be constantly stuck on social media. Distracker helps us track how much time we spend on distracting social media apps like Facebook, Twitter, and Instagram.
